Hi there! I’m Ulkar Jabbarli, a Master’s student in Computer Modeling of Physical Phenomena at the University of Warsaw. My research lies at the intersection of computational modeling, materials science, and condensed matter physics, with a particular focus on the study of 2D materials. Under the guidance of Dr. Magdalena Birowska, my current work investigates strain-induced effects in MnPX₃ materials (X=S, Se), exploring their implications on electronic structure and magnetism.
